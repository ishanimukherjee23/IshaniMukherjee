Research Interests

I study fish behavior, collective movement, and energetics under environmental change. My work focuses on how ecological pressures shape individual variation and group-level dynamics.

  • Collective behaviour in fish schools
  • Predator–prey interactions and escape dynamics
  • Energetics and metabolic costs of behaviour
  • Climate change and aquatic ecology
